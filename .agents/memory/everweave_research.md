# Everweave Research Notes
**Date:** 2026-05-12

## What Everweave Is
- AI DnD game by Endgame Studio Ltd (early access)
- AI acts as Game Master responding to player prompts
- Long-term campaign memory
- Character sheets with stats, abilities, inventory
- Quest generation, NPC dialogue

## What Everweave Does WELL
- Cinematic, story-driven AI Game Master
- Built-in D&D mechanics (dice rolls, stats)
- Long-term memory across sessions
- NPC character sheets with their own stats + inventory (roadmap)
- Companion character engine (planned)
- PC version shows item descriptions WITH effects and usage instructions
- Good music/atmosphere
- Campaign export (players can export their full story)

## What Everweave Does POORLY (Aurora must be better)
1. **Inventory is broken** — AI DM doesn't recognize items, game breaks over 50 items, no stacking, duplicate items
2. **No item filtering/closing** — popup blocks screen, no way to dismiss
3. **AI is a yes-man** — no real challenge or consequence, players can abuse it easily
4. **Combat doesn't use equipment** — stats/gear not factored in properly
5. **Player does all the heavy lifting** — AI doesn't proactively drive story, quests feel player-generated not world-generated
6. **Expensive and message-limited**
7. **Mobile vs PC inconsistency** — different features on each platform
8. **No life paths** — purely combat/adventure focused, no farmer/trader/scholar paths
9. **Backstory is ignored** — AI doesn't use character backstory meaningfully
10. **No death consequences** — easily cheesed

## Aurora Advantages to Build On
- Life-path mastery system (Aurora exclusive — farmer/scholar/trader paths)
- World-first god-game concept (design then LIVE in your world)
- Stat-driven outcomes (WIS farmers, CHA traders, INT scholars)
- Mastery that grows through USE not generic XP
- Inventory must be CLEAN: limited slots, item descriptions, effects shown, no popup spam
- Quest system must feel world-driven, not player-manufactured
- Death must have real weight and consequence
- Character backstory MUST influence the narrative

## Key Features Aurora Should Build Next
1. **Inventory system** — slots, weight classes, item effects visible, loot from exploration
2. **Quest log** — auto-detected + manually accepted quests with stages
3. **Death & Respawn** — consequences, not game-over. Lose items, wake somewhere new, reputation hit
4. **Shop/Trade system** — buy/sell with in-world currency, price influenced by CHA
5. **Companion system** (later) — NPCs with their own stats
6. **Campaign export** — let players save/share their story
